PART VII — AGENTS

Despite everything that had happened...

Despite surviving a fate that should have ended her existence...

Despite the protection that surrounded her...

Angelica never truly became part of Isla Isabela.

She lived upon the island.

She fought for it.

She learned its rules.

She obeyed them when necessary.

She broke them when she had no other choice.

But belonging and surviving are not the same thing.

No matter how many years passed, Isla Isabela never felt like home.

To Angelica, it remained exactly what it had always been.

A prison disguised as a civilization.

Escaping the island was never as simple as leaving its shores.

Every route was monitored.

Every port observed.

Every organization watched by another.

Information itself had become the island's most valuable currency.

Without it...

Freedom did not exist.

Angelica understood this long before anyone else.

Strength alone could never overcome an entire system.

Knowledge could.

So she built something far more dangerous than an army.

A network.

Those within the organization simply called them...

Agents.

An Agent was never defined by race.

Nor by nationality.

Nor by loyalty to any government.

The only requirement was usefulness.

Each Agent worked alone.

Each possessed a single responsibility.

Gather information.

Acquire resources.

Prepare for the day escape became possible.

Most Agents never met one another.

Many never even knew they served the same cause.

Isolation protected the network.

If one disappeared...

The others continued.

No single individual possessed the complete picture.

Only Angelica.

Her influence spread quietly through every layer of Isla Isabela.

Humans carried her messages without realizing where they ultimately led.

Half-Vampires secretly supplied weapons, medicine, and intelligence.

Members of rebellious organizations unknowingly advanced her objectives.

Even professional Hunters occasionally became pieces of a larger design without ever understanding whose game they had entered.

No institution was beyond her reach.

Every faction contained eyes loyal to Angelica.

Some out of respect.

Some because they owed her their lives.

Some for money.

Some simply because they believed the island deserved another future.

Motives differed.

Results did not.

The network expanded until it became impossible to measure.

Governments searched for leaders.

Guilds searched for conspiracies.

Authorities searched for organizations.

They found almost nothing.

Because there was nothing obvious to find.

No headquarters.

No banners.

No uniforms.

No official membership.

Only individuals...

Scattered throughout Isla Isabela...

Connected by fragments of trust invisible to everyone except the woman who had assembled them.

The greatest organizations often appear smaller than they truly are.

Angelica understood this better than anyone.

Visibility creates targets.

Silence creates survival.

Among those who crossed paths with Yamato, several were already part of this hidden network.

To him, they appeared ordinary.

Travelers.

Workers.

Informants.

Mercenaries.

Nothing more.

He never realized that some conversations had been carefully arranged before they ever occurred.

Some encounters were accidents.

Others had been waiting for him long before he arrived.

Such is the nature of Angelica's network.

One rarely knows an Agent is standing nearby...

Until long after the moment has passed.

Power on Isla Isabela has never belonged solely to those with the strongest bodies.

Nor to those carrying the oldest bloodlines.

Nor even to those blessed by Devils.

Real power belongs to those who possess information before everyone else.

Because knowledge travels faster than armies.

Faster than governments.

Faster than fear.

Angelica understood a truth most rulers never learn.

A kingdom may be built with soldiers.

An empire may be built with wealth.

But a future...

A future is built by those who already know where the world is going before anyone else does.

And so, while others fought for territory...

While vampires guarded their authority...

While humans struggled merely to survive...

Angelica quietly built something far more enduring.

Not an army.

Not a nation.

But a web of invisible threads stretching across every corner of Isla Isabela.

A web waiting for the day someone would finally dare to pull it.
